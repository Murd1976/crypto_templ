The 4x4 Pump Buy Signal Dev strategy with Candle TRAILING Stop-Loss

At any time, analyze last N candles
- IF 100-(100*([N close price]/[candle 1 open price])) > P
- THEN trigger BUY


NOTE:
- N, X, R, P are arbitrary values entered by user
- change is the difference between the candle close price and open price


EXAMPLE:
N=4 we instruct the bot to anaylize the last 4 candles
X=0
R=100
P=4
