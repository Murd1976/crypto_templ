The Curve Inversion strategy

At any time, analyze last N candles
- IF candles from 1 to N-X have at least R% of candles with change < 0
- and IF candles from (N-X)+1 to N have change > 0
- and IF 100-(100*([N close price]/[candle 1 open price])) < P
- THEN trigger BUY


NOTE:
- N, X, R, P are arbitrary values entered by user
- change is the difference between the candle close price and open price


EXAMPLE:
N=12 we instruct the bot to anaylize the last 12 candles
X=2
R=80
P=4


it is
- IF 80% of the candles from 1 to 10 [N-X, or 12-2] have change < 0 [meaning: if at least 8 candles of 10 are RED candles]
- and IF candles 11 [(N-X)+1, or (12-2)+1] to 12 have change >0 [meaning: last 2 candles are GREEN candles]
- and IF 100-(100*([candle 12 close price] / [candle 1 open price])) < 4 [meaning: from candle 1 to 12, the price has decreased more than 4%] - THEN trigger BUY THEN buy


Basic settings for this strategy


BUY SIGNAL : CURVE INVERSION

N=14 we instruct the bot to anaylize the last 14 candles
X=2
R=90 at least 90% of (14-2=12) candles are red, that means at least 11 candles are red
